Adventure Notes:
Session 1: A group of us offered to help the boatmaster to row through the night to arrive on time, and we were beset by river pirates. Some swam and climbed aboard the ship, and some shot arrows from the shore. Annoying Dwarf was one of them. The pirates weren't trying to kill (although they did kill the crows nest lookout, accidentally). The midget bard was able to stun the dwarf (no idea how), and then went around letting the animals loose. What an idiot.

The cat-lady figured out to throw the rug onto the fire (I have no idea how it started), and attacked one of the pirates with her claws!?!

I smashed a barrel of water over the head of some pirate, but for my troubles, I was stabbed and shot. Ouch. Trying to save my life, I prayed for "fog" and moved in between the cows (and made "mooing" sounds) to try to hide.

I guess the battle was going badly for the pirates because they decided to leave, all except the dwarf... who was still stunned.

They thought the boatmaster's extra gold (that he was going to pay us) was worth the effort.

I was badly hurt, so I went into the hold and prayed. After a while, I fell asleep, and the dear Mother still healed me, somewhat. I guess I wasn't as close to her as normal, but in any case, I still felt better.

Once we got to town, the bard heard about some inn with troubles... but I had to go find the druid circle. The ranger came with me, to pay her respects. I found the signs and spent the night praying. My brethren healed me up and I felt fine in the morning.

As I was leaving, "Mist" the High Druid, told about influx of Faerie and Undead.

I said I would investigate.
